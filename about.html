About Us

In today’s dynamic business environment, it is imperative that Finance and Accounting (F&A) functions evolve from being keepers of historical records to active participants in the business value-creation process, providing strategic decision support

SAK Acounting Finance and Accounting Outsourcing team helps businesses streamline their F&A processes, handle large volumes of data with accuracy, manage statutory compliances, achieve transformational objectives, and ensure timely closure of financial statements in accordance with required accounting standards. We generate real-time actionable insights for clients’ leadership and support them through the annual financial closure and audit activities.

Our team of qualified US CPA’s and Indian CPAs are well-versed in US-GAAP, IFRS, IND-AS and IGAAP, and is equipped to create an ecosystem for Finance Leaders to deploy efficient operations design, manage processes, and become strategic business partners. We offer a flexible engagement model tailored to the size and needs of the business. As every client is different from the other and so is their requirements, hence we try to understand the need of each client and deliver customized business support to them, varying from simple data entry transactions to complex transactions.
